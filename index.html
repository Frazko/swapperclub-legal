Privacy Policy

SwapperClub — Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Introduction

SwapperClub ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application for trading collectible stickers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name (alias), country, city, and zip code.

Profile photo: You may optionally take or choose a photo for your profile avatar. We access your device camera and photo library only when you explicitly choose to set a profile photo. Photos are stored securely in our cloud storage.

Collection data: Sticker inventory data you enter (which stickers you have, need, or want to trade).

Messages: Content of messages exchanged with other users through the in-app chat feature.

Usage data: Anonymous analytics events such as screen views, feature usage, and session duration to help us improve the app experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with other traders based on your sticker collection and location
  • To enable communication between traders
  • To display rankings and reputation scores
  • To improve the app through anonymous usage analytics

4. Camera & Photo Library

We request access to your device camera and photo library solely for the purpose of setting your profile avatar. We do not access your camera or photos for any other purpose. You can deny this permission and still use the app — you simply won't be able to set a custom profile photo.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your profile information (alias, country, city) is visible to other users for the purpose of facilitating trades. Your email address is never shared with other users.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with row-level security policies. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Upon account deletion, your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

SwapperClub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
