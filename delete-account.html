SwapperClub — Account & Data Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your SwapperClub account directly from the app by following these steps:

  1. Open SwapperClub and sign in to your account
  2. Go to Settings (tap the gear icon in the bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Read the confirmation warning and confirm your decision
  5. Your account will be permanently deleted
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to proceed before confirming.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your profile information (email, alias, country, city, zip code)
  • Your profile photo / avatar
  • Your sticker collection and inventory data
  • Your trade cards
  • Your trade requests and trade history
  • Your chat messages and conversations
  • Your community wall posts
  • Your ratings and reputation data
  • Your favorites list
  • Your premium subscription entitlements
  • Your analytics and usage data

What May Be Retained

  • Moderation records: Reports you filed or that were filed against you may be retained for safety and compliance purposes for up to 90 days after account deletion.
  • Aggregated analytics: Anonymous, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained as they cannot be linked back to your account.

Retention Period

Account deletion is processed immediately. All identifiable personal data is removed from our servers within 30 days of the deletion request.

Alternative: Contact Us

If you are unable to access the app to delete your account, you may request account deletion by email:

Email: privacy@swapperclub.com

Please include the email address associated with your SwapperClub account. We will process your request within 30 days.
